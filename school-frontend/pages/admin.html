📧 Email Notifications

Send overdue fee reminders to parents. Only parents with a linked account and email address will receive reminders.

Setup required: Add these to your Render environment variables:
  • SMTP_USER — your Gmail address
  • SMTP_PASS — your Gmail App Password
  • SMTP_HOST — smtp.gmail.com (default)
  • SMTP_PORT — 587 (default)

Test email will be sent to your own account to verify setup works.
