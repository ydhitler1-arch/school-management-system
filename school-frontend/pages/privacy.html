Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the School Management System ("the System") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information of students, teachers, and staff. By using this system, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

The System collects the following categories of personal data:

  • Student information: Name, age, class, roll number, attendance records, grades, and fee payment records.
  • Teacher information: Name, subject, email address, and login credentials.
  • Usage data: Login timestamps and actions performed within the system (for audit purposes).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage student enrolment, attendance, academic performance, and fee records.
  • To provide teachers and administrators access to relevant academic data.
  • To maintain accurate school records as required by educational regulations.
  • To contact teachers regarding their accounts and school-related matters.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas cloud servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing — plain text passwords are never stored.
  • Access to the system requires authentication via secure JWT tokens.
  • Role-based access control ensures users can only access data relevant to their role.
  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

4. Data Retention

Student and teacher records are retained for the duration of their association with the school and for a period of 5 years thereafter, as required by educational record-keeping regulations in India. Fee and attendance records are retained for 7 years for compliance purposes.

5. Who Has Access to Your Data

  • Administrators have access to all student, teacher, attendance, grade, and fee records.
  • Teachers have access to student data, attendance, and grades relevant to their classes.
  • No student or teacher data is shared with third parties except as required by law.

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act, India)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you or your child.
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Request erasure of personal data where it is no longer necessary.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (subject to legal record-keeping obligations).

To exercise these rights, contact your school administrator directly.

7. Cookies

The System uses browser localStorage to store authentication tokens. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the system after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact your school administrator.
